## Notes & Caveats

- **Date range is 9 days**, shorter than the default 90-day analysis. Windows are sub-divided into 3×3 days. Small sample sizes per window may amplify apparent variance.
- **Content segmentation** uses the MVP keyword classifier (case-insensitive partial match on title only). 51 of 118 Issues fall in "other"; a body-based or LLM classifier would reduce this.
- **`phase/done` label transition gap** (60 CLOSED without `phase/done`) should be tracked as a separate quality issue — it affects the strict First-try metric but not the practical completion view.
